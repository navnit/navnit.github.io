How it works

Open. Tune. Export.

  1. 1 — Open

    Drop in a local video. One screen, no timeline to build — your project autosaves as you work, and your original file stays read-only.

  2. 2 — Tune

    Drag the threshold; every proposed cut updates live on the timeline. Click any hatched cut to keep that section. Your original file is never modified.

  3. 3 — Export

    Render a finished MP4 that matches exactly what the Edited preview showed — nothing more to configure.

Use cases

Silence hides everywhere.

01 · Meetings & demos

The demo worked. The waiting didn't.

Screen shares booting, apps processing, "can everyone see my screen?" — recorded meetings are full of dead stretches. Gapless finds each quiet stretch and closes it, so the recording you share is just the demo.

02 · Screencasts & tutorials

Narrate at your own pace.

Pause between sentences. Check your notes. Re-find your cursor. Record naturally — then set the threshold and everything quieter than it gets trimmed, with a margin around every cut so the edit breathes.

03 · Podcasts & interviews

Keep the conversation. Lose the lag.

Guests think before they answer — sometimes for a while. Gapless tightens long pauses to a natural beat without touching a single word, keeping a sliver of silence on each side so it never sounds clipped.

04 · Lectures & talks

Don't cut. Fast-forward.

Sometimes silence is context — writing on a whiteboard, a class working through a problem. Switch from cutting to fast-forwarding and quiet stretches play at 8× instead of disappearing. Nothing is lost, just compressed.

Why Gapless

Automatic, but never out of your hands.

Review every cut

Every proposed cut is visible on the timeline before you export — nothing disappears without you seeing it.

Restore any section

A dramatic pause worth keeping? Click the cut to put it back. The edit is non-destructive; your original file is untouched.

Fast-forward instead of deleting

When silence is context, compress it to 8× instead of removing it. Nothing is lost.

Natural speech margins

A configurable margin stays on both sides of every cut, so sentences never sound clipped.

Autosaved projects

Every change autosaves to a small .gapless project file — close the app mid-edit, reopen, and your cuts and overrides are exactly where you left them.

Fully local

Everything runs on your machine. No upload, no account, no watermark — your recordings stay yours.

Why Gapless exists

I was demoing our product on a call. Every time I clicked Run, everyone sat and watched a progress bar crawl. The recording came out 22 minutes long — for about nine minutes of actual demo. Cutting the waiting out by hand took longer than the meeting itself.

Gapless is the tool I wanted that afternoon: open the recording, review the cuts, keep the nine minutes that matter. It's free, open-source, and built as the visual interface for the excellent auto-editor engine.
